Ingredients

1 lb pasta

1 tbsp olive oil

2 cloves garlic,minced

1 can(28 oz)crushed tomatoes

1 cup heavy cream

1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

Step 1

Cook pasta according to package instructions.Drain and set aside.

Step 2

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.

Step 3

Stir in crushed tomotoes,heavy cream,and parmesan cheese.Season with salt and pepper.

Step 4

Bring the sauce to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes,stirring occasionally,until slightly thickened.

Step 5

Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly

Step 6

Garnish with free basil before serving.

Recipe Details

Cooking Time

30 minutes

Difficulty

Easy

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories:450,Fat:20g,Protein:15g
